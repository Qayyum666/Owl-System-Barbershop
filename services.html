Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How long does each service take?

Service durations vary: Haircuts take 30 minutes, Beard Trims take 20 minutes, Combo services take 45 minutes, and Premium Packages take 60 minutes. Please arrive 5 minutes before your appointment.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot. You can book online up to 30 days in advance.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled time without any penalty. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a small fee.

Do you offer services for children?

Yes! We offer special kids' haircut services with experienced barbers who are great with children. We also provide fun seating options and entertainment to make their experience enjoyable.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments. You can also pay through our mobile app or website when booking your appointment.
